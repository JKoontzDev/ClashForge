Information we may collect

ClashForge may collect information you enter into the site, such as fighter data, battle actions, and bug report submissions. If you send a bug report, you may choose to include your name, email address, the affected page URL, a summary, and details.

How we use information

We use submitted information to run the app, show public game content, troubleshoot issues, improve reliability, and respond to bug reports when contact information is provided.

Cookies and session data

The site uses standard Django session and CSRF cookies to keep basic browser state and protect forms from cross-site request forgery. These cookies are used for security and site functionality.

Logs and security monitoring

Like most web apps, the hosting stack and application may keep request logs for operations, abuse prevention, debugging, and security monitoring. Logs may include technical details such as request time, requested URL, IP address, user agent, and error information.

Bug report submissions

Bug reports are stored so the site owner can review and fix issues. Each report also stores the server-side app version and build version active when the report was submitted. Do not include passwords, secrets, or sensitive personal information in bug reports.

Third-party hosting and infrastructure

ClashForge may run on third-party hosting, database, caching, logging, or infrastructure providers. Those providers process data as needed to operate the site.

Contact

For privacy questions or requests, contact the site owner through the public contact channel listed for ClashForge or its hosting domain.

Policy updates

This policy may be updated as the app changes. The latest version will be posted on this page.
